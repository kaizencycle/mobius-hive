# Mobius HIVE — Token Model v0.1

## Purpose

HIVE needs an economy that feels:
- civic
- visible
- ritualized
- meaningful

It should not feel like a generic monetization layer.

---

## Core principle

**Production alone should not be enough.**

Value should flow toward:
- contribution
- stewardship
- trust
- repair
- teaching
- governance participation
- world-improving action

One-line canon:

> **If labor is automated, meaning becomes the new economy.**

---

## Economic layers

## 1. Utility layer
This is the resource layer used for:
- transactions
- services
- market functions
- quest unlocks
- district actions

Purpose:
- make the world usable
- support everyday flow

---

## 2. Governance layer
This is the value committed to:
- proposals
- oath pools
- elder support / opposition
- festival voting
- civic legitimacy

Purpose:
- make governance costly and real

---

## 3. Integrity layer
This is the part of the economy tied to:
- trust signals
- civic reliability
- meaningful contribution
- verified system-improving behavior

Purpose:
- reward actions that preserve the civilization shell

---

## Economic verbs

Players should experience the economy through verbs, not abstractions.

### Earn
- complete learning loops
- repair public systems
- teach others
- verify or support civic processes
- complete quests and festivals

### Spend
- acquire tools, access, upgrades, event items, functional services

### Stake
- support proposals
- support elders
- commit to institutions

### Burn
- ritual actions
- ascension attempts
- festival oaths
- scarcity maintenance

### Lock
- governance deposits
- throne mechanics
- seasonal civic commitments

---

## World-facing economy rule

The economy must always show up in-world.

Examples:
- market surge = Quarter becomes brighter and busier
- governance staking = oath pools visibly fill
- burns = ritual flames intensify
- upgrades = workshops, homes, or halls physically improve
- stagnation = stalls dim, districts feel sparse

---

## Anti-failure rules

The economy should avoid becoming:
- pure speculation
- pay-to-win dominance
- obedience scoring
- empty number farming
- prestige without contribution

### Guardrails
- contribution should matter more than extraction
- public goods should have reward loops
- reputation / integrity should not equal total human worth
- economy should be challengeable and auditable

---

## Canon sentence

**HIVE’s economy should reward not just what is produced, but what is produced in a way that keeps the world worth living in.**
