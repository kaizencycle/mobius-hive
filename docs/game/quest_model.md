# HIVE — Quest Model v1

## Purpose

This document defines quests inside HIVE.

Quests are not disposable tasks.

Every quest should:
- affect the civilization
- alter legitimacy
- influence the ledger
- reshape memory
- or permanently modify the world state.

One-line canon:

> In HIVE, quests become history.

---

# Quest Categories

## Scout Quests

Exploration and discovery.

Examples:
- shard hunts
- mirror expeditions
- biome recon missions
- crown fragment recovery
- collapse investigations.

Scout quests generate:
- lore
- map visibility
- shard recovery
- civilization expansion.

---

## Citizen Quests

Society maintenance and governance.

Examples:
- Festival participation
- Oath Pool voting
- public works
- reconstruction events
- economic stabilization.

Citizen quests generate:
- legitimacy
- morale
- infrastructure health
- social cohesion.

---

## Agent Quests

Infrastructure and system-level tasks.

Examples:
- archive maintenance
- market balancing
- signal routing
- corruption containment
- anomaly investigation.

These quests are tied to Agent institutions.

---

## Elder Quests

High-stakes governance gameplay.

Examples:
- edict proposals
- throne defense
- diplomatic summits
- challenge rituals
- civilization stabilization.

Failure weakens legitimacy.
Success permanently alters civilization trajectory.

---

## Reflection Quests

Identity and psychological quests.

Examples:
- memory reconstruction
- Spectral Teacher encounters
- identity trials
- continuity rituals
- soul reflection events.

These quests permanently branch player development.

---

## World Quests

Server-wide civilization events.

Examples:
- Dome reconstruction
- biome synchronization
- orbital expansion
- reality fractures
- collapse simulations.

These quests permanently modify world state.

---

# Quest Philosophy

Most games treat quests as:
- rewards
- progression systems
- disposable content.

HIVE treats quests as:
- civilization memory
- governance pressure
- historical causality.

The world remembers completed quests.

That memory becomes future terrain.
