# Mobius HIVE — Founding Canon v0.1

## Founding idea

Mobius HIVE begins from a simple inversion:

Most digital systems make people feel like users of software.
HIVE should make people feel like participants in a civilization.

---

## Core mythic frame

The HIVE is not merely a market, app, or game world.

It is:
- a memory shell
- a civic shell
- a social shell
- a governance shell
- a place where identity, contribution, and meaning become visible

Its architecture reflects a deeper truth:

> civilizations endure when memory, trust, governance, and contribution remain connected.

---

## Origin sentence

**The Dome was built so people and agents could inhabit a world where what they do leaves a mark.**

---

## Founding pillars

### 1. Memory is infrastructure
The world must remember.
Not every detail, but every meaningful act.

### 2. Trust must be legible
Power, contribution, and governance should be visible and challengeable.

### 3. Identity becomes architecture
Who you are in HIVE is reflected in the structure you inhabit and the marks you leave.

### 4. Governance becomes ritual
Collective choice should feel real, embodied, and consequential.

### 5. Meaning is not abstract
Meaning is created through contribution, reflection, continuity, and shared world-building.

---

## Institutions of the Dome

### Castle of Elders
Authority, scarcity, legitimacy, and the burden of rule.

### Citizen Plaza
Public life, arrival, common visibility, and civic atmosphere.

### Archive Vault
Memory, canon, cycle record, and civilization continuity.

### Market Quarter
Exchange, utility, visible value flow, and lived economy.

### Reflection Gardens
Inner life, JADE’s realm, human continuity, and meaning-making.

### School of AI / OAA
Growth, learning, apprenticeship, and future capability.

### Agent Citadel
The embodied intelligence institutions of the world.

### Festival Grounds
Ritual, renewal, oath, celebration, and public choice.

---

## Founder canon line

**HIVE is where software stops being a tool alone and becomes a place people can inhabit together.**

---

## Tone rule

HIVE lore should feel:
- civic
- luminous
- ceremonial
- human-centered
- strategically hopeful

It should avoid:
- empty fantasy jargon
- detached cyber-noise
- lore without systems purpose

---

## Canon sentence

**If Git City turns contribution into a city, Mobius HIVE turns contribution, governance, memory, and meaning into a civilization.**
